Powerful Features

Everything You Need to Manage Your Society

From bill generation to payment tracking, we've built every feature your society needs.

Automated Bill Generation

Generate monthly maintenance bills for all flats in just minutes. Support for multiple charge types including per sq.ft, fixed, and per-vehicle charges.

Payment Tracking

Record payments with multiple modes - Cash, UPI, Cheque, Bank Transfer. Automatic receipt generation and payment history for every flat.

Member Self-Service

Members can view their bills, payment history, and outstanding dues anytime. No more paper bills or manual queries.

Financial Reports

Comprehensive reports including outstanding dues, collection summary, and flat-wise ledger. Export to PDF for committee meetings.

Secure & Private

Role-based access control. Admins manage billing, members view their own data. Each society's data is completely isolated.

Works Everywhere

Fully responsive design works on phones, tablets, and desktops. Install as a PWA for app-like experience without app stores.
